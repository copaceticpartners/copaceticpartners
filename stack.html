On AI

Nobody fully understands what's coming. That's not a reason to wait — it's a reason to move.

AI is unpredictable, fast-moving, and genuinely hard to keep up with. But the people who figure out how to use it intelligently — not to replace their judgment, but to extend it — are going to own the next decade of business. The ones who wait for certainty will watch it happen from the sideline.

The goal is not to become an AI expert. The goal is to build a life where the system does the heavy lifting so you can be fully present for everything else. The deals. The relationships. The moments that actually matter.

Most people are using AI like a smarter search engine. A small group is using it to build automated pipelines that run while they sleep, agents that manage their inbox, and systems that compound without them. That gap is the biggest business opportunity available right now.
