The Stack
8 Tools. $214 a Month.
01
Apollo.io
Source verified contacts at scale. Filter by job title, fund type, AUM, and location. We pulled 91,000 qualified GP and family office targets in the fund space alone. Export 500+ qualified leads in minutes.
02
Clay
Run every contact through waterfall enrichment. Verify work emails. Score intent signals. What comes out is a precision-targeted list — not a raw dump. Never pay per-row verification in Instantly — Clay handles it upstream.
03
Claude
Feed it the fund details, the investor profile, and your voice. It writes personalized outreach that reads one-to-one. Not templates. Actual copy that converts. Also handles operations audits, modernization roadmaps, and deal analysis.
04
Instantly.ai
1,000 emails a month while you sleep. Handles warmup, throttling, and deliverability automatically. Text-only delivery for maximum inbox placement. Traditional IR teams charge $2,000 per meeting booked. This books them for under $20.
05
Manus
Level 3 agent — give it a task and walk away. Weekly Apollo searches, performance reports, investor research, AI news briefs. It builds the list, writes the report, and sends it to you. Zero manual input required.
06
OpenClaw
The agent that never sleeps. Monitors your inbox 24/7, categorizes every reply, drafts follow-ups before you wake up, and updates Airtable automatically. The pipeline runs without you.
07
Zapier

The Workflow
How It Runs End to End
  • 01
    Apollo finds 500 qualified contacts and exports them as a CSV. Filter by title, industry, fund type, and location.
  • 02
    Clay enriches every contact, verifies the work email, and flags high-intent targets. Upload Clay's Work Email column to Instantly — not Apollo's original email field.
  • 03
    Claude writes a 4-email sequence personalized to the fund and investor profile. Each email handles one objection — security, protection, alignment, urgency.
  • 04
    Instantly sends the sequence on a M–F schedule, 25 emails a day, fully automated. Text-only delivery. Open and click tracking off for maximum deliverability.
  • 05
    OpenClaw monitors every reply in real time. Categorizes interest level, drafts follow-up responses, and flags hot leads for your approval before anything sends.
  • 06
    Zapier logs every Calendly booking to Airtable automatically. Contact details, call status, and capital committed — tracked without touching a spreadsheet.
  • 07
    Manus runs weekly reporting — pipeline analysis, reply rate trends, sequence optimization suggestions. Sent to your inbox every Monday morning.
  • 08
    You show up to the call. That's it. The system fills the calendar. You close the deal.
