Play 04
The Double Asset Play

The boomer who owns both the building and the business inside it. A property management company with 300 units where the owner also owns six of those buildings. Two appreciating assets for the price of one conversation. First-time homebuyer average age is now 40 — up from 33 five years ago. Home prices are 6x median household income. Institutional investors own 30% of all single family homes. The people who can't afford the house can afford the business that finances, sells, and manages them.

How to Find Double Asset Plays
Apollo
Search property management company owners who are also listed as real estate investors. The overlap in title and industry filters surfaces the double asset opportunity.
Perplexity
Research the owner's public real estate holdings. Property records, LLC filings, county assessor data. Most own more than they advertise in any business listing.
Claude
Build the combined valuation model. Business at current multiple plus real estate at market value. Present both as a single acquisition conversation. Nobody else is doing this.
2x
Assets acquired in one deal. The business generates cash flow. The real estate appreciates. The owner wanted out of both and didn't know how to sell them together. You did.
